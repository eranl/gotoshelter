**Privacy Policy for GoToShelter** Last Updated: 3/17/26
1. **Information Collection** Go To Shelter ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle.

    • **Location Data**: The App requests "Approximate Location" to determine if a received emergency alert is relevant to 
   your current area. This data is processed locally on your device and is **never** uploaded to any server.
    
    • **Notifications**: The App monitors system notifications to detect emergency alerts. This is used solely to 
   trigger the navigation feature.
2. **Data Sharing** We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. All processing happens on-device.
3. **Open Source** The App is open-source. You can verify our data handling practices by reviewing the source code at: https://github.com/eranl/gotoshelter
4. **Third-Party Services** The App may launch Waze or Google Maps for navigation. These services have their own privacy policies.
