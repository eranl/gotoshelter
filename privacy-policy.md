**Privacy Policy for GoToShelter**
Last Updated: 3/17/26

**1. Independent Tool & Government Disclaimer**
GoToShelter is an independent, open-source application and is NOT affiliated with, authorized by, or endorsed by any
government entity. The app provides supplementary safety information by monitoring official notification channels, such
as the Israel Home Front Command (https://www.oref.org.il/).

**2. Information Collection & Privacy**
GoToShelter is designed with privacy as a core principle.

• **Location Data**: The App requests "Approximate Location" to determine if a received emergency alert is relevant to
your current area. This data is processed strictly locally on your device and is **never** uploaded, stored, or shared
with any third party or server.

• **Notifications**: The App monitors system notifications to detect emergency alerts. This is used solely to trigger
the navigation feature locally.

**3. Data Sharing**
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. All data processing occurs on your
device.

**4. Open Source**
The App is fully open-source. Users and auditors can verify our data handling and privacy practices by reviewing the
source code at: https://github.com/eranl/gotoshelter

**5. Third-Party Services**
The App launches third-party navigation apps (Waze or Google Maps) to provide directions. These services operate under
their own respective privacy policies and terms of service.

**6. No Representation of Government Services**
This app is a supplementary tool and should be used alongside official government alerts and instructions. It does not
facilitate or provide official government services.
